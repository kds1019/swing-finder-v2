# 🚀 New Features Guide

## Three Major Improvements Added

---

## 1️⃣ **Earnings Filter** ⚠️

### **What It Does:**
- Shows earnings warnings on every scanner card
- Filters out stocks with upcoming earnings
- Helps you avoid gap risk from earnings surprises

### **How to Use:**

#### **In Scanner Sidebar:**
```
☑️ Exclude stocks with earnings in next 7 days
```

#### **On Scanner Cards:**
You'll now see earnings warnings:
- 🔴 **Earnings in 0-2 days** (high risk - avoid!)
- 🟡 **Earnings in 3-7 days** (caution)
- 🟢 **Earnings in 8-30 days** (lower risk)

#### **Example:**
```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ AAPL - $155.00                  │
│ ⭐ Smart: 85                     │
│ 💎 Discount Entry (38% Fib)     │
│ 🔥 Technology                   │
│ 🟡 Earnings in 3-7 days         │  ← NEW!
│ Setup: Breakout                 │
│ 🛡️ Stop: $150.00                │
│ 🎯 Target: $165.00              │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

### **Why It Matters:**
- ❌ **Before:** You might enter AAPL, then earnings surprise gaps it down 10%
- ✅ **After:** You see the warning and skip it or wait until after earnings

## 2️⃣ **Sector Rotation Enhancements** 📊

### **What It Does:**
- Shows sector badge on every scanner card
- Highlights hot sectors with 🔥 emoji
- Filter to only show stocks in hot sectors

### **How to Use:**

#### **In Scanner Sidebar:**
```
🧠 Enable Smart Mode ☑️
🔥 Only show stocks in hot sectors ☑️
```

#### **On Scanner Cards:**
You'll now see sector badges:
- 🔥 **Technology** (hot sector - positive momentum)
- 📊 **Energy** (neutral/cold sector)

#### **Example:**
```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ MSFT - $370.00                  │
│ ⭐ Smart: 92                     │
│ 💎 Discount Entry (42% Fib)     │
│ 🔥 Technology                   │  ← NEW! (Hot sector)
│ Setup: Pullback                 │
│ 🛡️ Stop: $365.00                │
│ 🎯 Target: $380.00              │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

### **Why It Matters:**
- ✅ Trade with sector momentum (higher win rate)
- ✅ Avoid weak sectors (lower risk)
- ✅ See at a glance which stocks are in strong sectors

## 3️⃣ **Mistake Tracker** 🚨

### **What It Does:**
- Tracks mistakes when you close losing trades
- Shows mistake stats in Journal
- Helps you identify patterns and improve

### **How to Use:**

#### **When Closing a Trade:**
1. Go to **Active Trades**
2. Click **"❌ Close"** on any position
3. Enter exit price and reason
4. **NEW:** If it's a loss, you'll see:

```
🚨 Mistake Tracker (Optional)
What went wrong? (Select if applicable)

[Dropdown with options:]
- None - Followed my plan perfectly
- Entered too early
- Entered too late
- Didn't follow my stop
- Moved my stop (gave it more room)
- Took profit too early
- Held past my target (greed)
- Ignored my rules
- Traded on emotion
- Position size too large
- Traded during earnings
- Traded against the trend
- FOMO trade (no setup)
- Revenge trading
- Other
```

5. Select the mistake (if any)
6. Click **"✅ Save to Journal"**

#### **In Journal Page:**
Go to **Journal** → **Performance Stats** tab

You'll see:
```
🚨 Mistake Tracker

Total Mistakes Tracked: 12
Cost of Mistakes: -$2,450.00
Most Common Mistake: Entered too early (5x)

📊 Mistake Breakdown:
- Entered too early: 5 times → Cost: -$1,200.00
- Didn't follow my stop: 3 times → Cost: -$800.00
- Traded on emotion: 2 times → Cost: -$300.00
- FOMO trade (no setup): 2 times → Cost: -$150.00
```

### **Why It Matters:**
- 📊 **Data-driven improvement** - See exactly what's costing you money
- 🎯 **Focus your efforts** - Work on your #1 mistake first
- 📈 **Track progress** - Watch mistakes decrease over time
- 💡 **Pattern recognition** - "I always enter too early on breakouts"

## 🎯 **How These Work Together:**

### **Example Workflow:**

1. **Scanner finds AAPL**
   - ⭐ Smart Score: 85
   - 🔥 Technology (hot sector) ✅
   - 🟡 Earnings in 5 days ⚠️
   - **Decision:** Skip it (earnings too soon)

2. **Scanner finds MSFT**
   - ⭐ Smart Score: 92
   - 🔥 Technology (hot sector) ✅
   - No earnings for 30+ days ✅
   - **Decision:** Enter the trade!

3. **Trade goes against you**
   - Close at a loss
   - Mistake Tracker: "Entered too early"
   - Saved to journal

4. **Review in Journal**
   - See you've "entered too early" 5 times
   - Cost: -$1,200
   - **Action:** Focus on waiting for confirmation

5. **Next Week**
   - Use earnings filter ✅
   - Use sector filter ✅
   - Wait for confirmation ✅
   - Win rate improves! 🎉
